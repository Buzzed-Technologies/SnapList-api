At SnapList, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

We regularly review our security practices to ensure the ongoing confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your data.
